Feature List

Master Budget

Monthly expenses that repeat are set as rollover values. A housekeeping budget feature allows a portion of income to be assigned.

The benefit being that monthly inevitable costs are deducted from Income from day 1 in the month keeping the disposable income in focus from payday.

How was your day?

A pulse radiator with a range of one word responses but also the option to provide more words why?

Considering expanding the feature to enable family members to record their perception of other members days.

Likes and dislikes feature

Currently records likes and dislikes of living in one country or another but this feature should be expanded to make it possible to set different topics.

Life Hacks

Life is not easy we all have similar but different challenges, often we forget to share our solutions with each other and life hacks is where we hope this will happen.

Thoughts

Thoughts are like footprints in the sand, leave a few behind so that you can revisit them from time to time.

People

The people in our lives help us grow and stop us from becoming isolated and disconnected. People can also be challenging, this feature helps to keep an eye on the way people are treating us.
